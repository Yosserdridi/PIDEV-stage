ESPRIT

Private Higher School of Engineering and Technology

The Private Higher School of Engineering and Technology or ESPRIT is a private engineering school in Tunisia based in Ariana and accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. ESPRIT also has a business school branch, the ESPRIT Business School.
